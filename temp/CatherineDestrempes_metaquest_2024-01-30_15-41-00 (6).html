About the Research Project

Please describe the work that has been carried out within your project. Fill only what is relevant for your project.

Title: Using old fields for new purposes: modelling the impact of field restoration in the Montérégie
Location: Montérégie, Landscape 2, Canada
Start Date: 2020-09-03
End Date: 2024-08-31
Abstract: Efforts to secure food sources through agriculture's expansion and industrialization have diminished landscape diversity, increasing vulnerability. While COP15 goals stress global natural space restoration, agricultural restoration creates a trade-off by reducing areas for crop production. However, utilizing unproductive lands like abandoned or degraded fields offers a potential solution. Using the agriculturally intensive Montérégie region as my model system, I will explore scenarios restoring 0-100% of all unproductive land and forecast the impact on seven ecosystem services (crop production, maple syrup, deer hunting, water regulation, carbon storage, recreation, pollination).I expect most of the impact to come from the amount of land restored and will focus on the importance of carefully planning restoration actions to maximize benefits for both people and nature.
